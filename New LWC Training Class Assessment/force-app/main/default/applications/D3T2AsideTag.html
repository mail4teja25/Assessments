The Cows and the Lions

Five Cows lived in a little forest. They ate fresh grass in a large green meadow.They were kind friends. They decided to do everthing together, So the lions couldn't attack them for food.

Moral of the Story

Unity is Strength

One day, the five cows fought and each one started to eat grass in a different place.The lions decided to seize the opportunity and kill them one by one.
